# About, license, and originality

## Mission

Provide a **free, updatable, original** machine learning and AI curriculum centered
on neurology and stroke—accurate methods, clinical decision framing, and no
dependence on copying commercial book wording or publisher figures.

## Originality bar (hard rules)

| Allowed | Forbidden |
|---------|-----------|
| Original teaching prose | Substantial similarity to commercial textbook prose |
| Synthetic clinical vignettes | Real patient stories / PHI |
| Code-drawn original figures | Lifted paper or book figures |
| Original teaching tables | Guideline instrument dumps; paper result tables |
| Named standards + links | Pasted full copyrighted checklists/instruments |
| Primary-source citations | Paper abstracts pasted as content |
| Synthetic worked numbers | Publisher PDF text |

This Web Edition was **adapted and rewritten** from an internal ML curriculum project
for web publication. Expression, vignettes, exercises, and figure assets are authored
for this free edition. Curriculum *topic coverage* may overlap any good ML course;
**wording and visuals do not copy commercial books.**

## Accuracy stance

- Prediction ≠ causation unless the design supports causal claims  
- Prefer calibration, external validation, and decision impact over AUROC theater  
- Recompute worked examples; do not trust marketing demos  
- Data leakage, site shift, and label noise matter as much as architecture novelty  

## Disclaimer

Educational material only. Authors make no warranty of completeness or fitness for
any clinical decision. You must verify against primary literature and local policy.
