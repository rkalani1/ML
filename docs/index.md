## What this is

A **Web Edition** teaching book for neurologists, stroke clinicians, and
computationally minded trainees who need practical ML literacy—mathematical
foundations, supervised and unsupervised learning, deep learning, reinforcement
learning, data engineering, model appraisal, and clinical deployment judgment.

## What this is not

- Not a digitized commercial ML textbook  
- Not a paste of paper abstracts, guideline instrument tables, or publisher PDFs  
- Not institutional UW/HMC policy  
- Not a device or software endorsement  

Teaching figures, when present, are **original** (code-drawn for this curriculum).
DOCX-embedded publisher or third-party images are **not** shipped on this site.
Methods standards (TRIPOD+AI, CONSORT-AI, DECIDE-AI, etc.) are referenced **by name**
with links to their home organizations—not reproduced as copyrighted instruments.
